Frequently Asked Questions

  • Netflix is a subscription-based streaming service that allows our members to watch TV shows and movies on an internet-connected device. Depending on your plan, you can also download TV shows and movies to your iOS, Android, or Windows 10 device and watch without an internet connection.

  • The mobile plan costs Rs 149 per month, while the basic plan is priced at Rs 199 a month. The Standard and Premium plan is for Rs 499 and Rs 649, respectively. The Basic plan (for those who keep it) streams in just HD (720p) resolution. The most expensive option, the Premium plan, can tap into content in 4K Ultra HD. Netflix remains one of the few streaming video services that require a more expensive plan for features like 4K, HDR, and Dolby Atmos.

  • Netflix is available in over 190 countries around the world. Each country has its own catalog of original and licensed TV shows and movies. The country on your account can't be changed unless you move to a new one.

  • To search TV shows and movies, look for the Search icon Search icon in the Netflix app and then enter your search term. Your homepage shows rows of TV shows and movies we think you’ll like. You can narrow these suggestions by selecting TV Shows or Movies from the menu. You can further narrow by Genres or Categories. The name of this selection, and its values, may vary between devices.
